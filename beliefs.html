Meet Marquis

Leadership

To me, leadership means taking control and guiding your team to success. The leader is supposed to aid their team and answer any questions that the team might have. The leader is to do what they think is best for their team.

Teamwork

Teamwork means working together and trying to accomplish the goal at the same time. Teamwork is supposed to make accomplishing the goal easier.
